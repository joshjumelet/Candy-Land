CANDY LAND

Are you ready to start your own Candy Land adventure?

Game Play

Travel through Candy Land along the path of colored spaces. The moves are made according to colored squares drawn by the players. Be the first player to reach the Candy Castle by reaching the final space at the end of the path.

  1. The youngest player goes first. Play then passes to the left.
  2. On your turn, draw one card from the deck and move your character to the corresponding next colored space. Your turn is over.
  3. The game is complete once a player has reached the final space on the board.
